Synthetic Fuel Initiative

  • Project Vision

    The synthetic fuel initiative aims to develop state-of-the-art synthetic fuel production facilities, invest in R&D for advanced synthetic fuel technologies, adapt existing infrastructure for storage and distribution, and establish strategic partnerships with technology providers and renewable energy companies.

  • High-level Objectives

    Production Facility: Develop a modern synthetic fuel production facility.

    R&D Investment: Invest in research and development to advance synthetic fuel technologies.

    Infrastructure Adaptation: Adapt current infrastructure for effective storage and distribution of synthetic fuels.

    Strategic Partnerships: Form partnerships with technology providers and renewable energy companies.

  • SWOT Analysis

    Strengths: Outstanding financial performance with a record EBITDA of $11 billion in 2023. Extensive and diversified energy infrastructure network.

    Weaknesses: Risks associated with large-scale, technically complex projects. Revenue limited within North America.

    Opportunities: Increased market scope by expanding infrastructure on natural gas. Investment in clean energy projects.

    Threats:Changing competition and technology. Stricter environmental regulations.
